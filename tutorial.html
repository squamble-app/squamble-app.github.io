Intro to Squamble

Squamble is a Discord bot based on a points system (CREDS)! You can get CREDS by just talking in the server. CREDS are shared through all servers. You can use CREDS to play games, steal creds from others, and more!

getcreds

/getcreds [user]

getcreds tell you how many CREDS [user] has!

makefarm

/makefarm

/makefarm lets you make a farm for a whopping 5000 CREDS! Farms increase your CREDBONUS

CREDBONUS

Your CREDBONUS increases the amount of CREDS you get from a message

steal

/steal [user]

/steal lets you steal 15 CREDS from [user]. It costs 10 CREDS to prepare. If [user] does NOT have 15 or more CREDS you lose your preparation cost. You have a 25% chance of getting caught. When getting caught you have to pay 30 CREDS in bail. (this is currently the only way to get negative creds)
